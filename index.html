A Little about Me

I am William Andres Romero, but now it is Romero. At least that is what everyone now calls me in college. I went to Woodward Academy and live right by the school in College Park, GA. I learned a lot in high school, and I had many leadership positions. I was lucky enough to get a bid from Sigma Chi at UGA, and pledged the first couple months. I did not have time to apply to be part of student government, but I plan on doing it next year. I really enjoy being involved with every organization as I was my pledge class treasurer, and I plan on trying to be a part of IFC and many more organizations while attending UGA. I am an intended-fiance major and plan on attending the Terry Business School. I have really enjoyed attending UGA thus far, and I can not wait what my future holds for me here at this university.

Sports

 Throughout my entire life, I have loved to play sports. I have played soccer, hockey, lacrosse, football, cross country, and wrestled. My main sport throughout middle and high school was lacrosse. I was the starting Varisty goalie by the end of my freshman year, and I was a captain my sophomore to senior year. Although I have played lacrosse most, my favorite pick up sport to play is soccer. I really enjoy the simplicity of the sport, and the fact that you can play soccer almost anywhere. I play pick up soccer with my pledge brothers usually 3 times a week, and the ingenious invention of the game, soccer tennis, has contributed to hours of the fun.
